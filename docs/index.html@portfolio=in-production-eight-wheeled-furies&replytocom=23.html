Four-Wheeled Furies

Posted in

Four-Wheeled Furies is a one-hour documentary about the women of the Rideau Valley Roller Girls (RVRG) and the fascinating world of roller derby.

The documentary explores roller derby through the eyes of a diverse group of women in the RVRG league. From newbie skaters (aka “Fresh Meat”) to seasoned veterans, the interview subjects cover the highs and lows of this increasingly popular sport, including the history of RVRG, the complex rules, and gruelling physical toll of the game, the women-positive atmosphere, and their commitment to giving back to the local community.

The documentary appeared on the Canadian Broadcasting Corporation’s (CBC) Absolutely Ottawa, for television broadcast on  4 August 2012 and 9/14/15 September 2013. It also appeared nationally on CBC’s Absolutely Canadian on 31 August 2013, 30 March 2013, and on the Digital Documentary channel on 8 November 2012
