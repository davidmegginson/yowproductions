Cara Rowlands, Animator
Home Our Team Cara Rowlands, Animator

Cara Rowlands received her training in traditional animation at Algonquin College. Since then, she has worked on animation projects including Stella & Sam for Mercury Filmworks, as well as the opening credits for Sweet Tarts Takeaway, and the production titles for Batturtle Productions. In addition to her animation projects, Cara has created a number of posters for community events, including roller derby posters for both the Rideau Valley Roller Girls and the Capital City Derby Dolls and the posters for both the 2010 and 2011 Lumiere Festival.

View her animation, illustration and graphic design work at www.cararowlands.com.
